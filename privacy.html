Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

AOLTD INTERACTIVE ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy applies to all AOLTD games, websites, and services, including CUBLY, Bones & Banner, TouchV2, The Accord, and any future releases.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on which game or service you use, we may collect:

  • Username, profile name, and email address (for account creation)
  • Login credentials (securely handled via PlayFab)
  • Game progress, achievements, and multiplayer data
  • Device information (model, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Approximate location (country-level only)
  • Optional gameplay and usage analytics
  • Advertising data (only when you choose to view rewarded ads)

Guest accounts are available in some games but may restrict access to online features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Save and sync game progress across devices
  • Enable multiplayer and social features
  • Display optional rewarded advertisements (with your consent)
  • Improve gameplay through analytics
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

3. Third-Party Services

Our games use trusted third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies:

  • PlayFab: Account management, data storage, and backend services
  • Advertising Partners: For optional rewarded ads (only when you choose to view them)

4. User-Generated Content

Players may create text-based content including chat messages, profile names, and clan names. You are responsible for what you share. We reserve the right to remove content that violates our Terms of Use.

5. Data Sharing & Analytics

We may share anonymized gameplay and device data with third-party services (PlayFab, analytics partners) to improve our games and ensure smooth operation. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account or personal data at any time by contacting us. We'll handle requests in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll notify users via in-game messages, email, or store listings. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests:
Email: aoltdinquiry@outlook.com

Governing Law: Tanzania (with worldwide applicability). Local laws may provide additional protections.

This policy applies to all AOLTD games and services. Each game will clearly inform you what data it collects before you play.
