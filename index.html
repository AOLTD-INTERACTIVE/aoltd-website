AOLTD: CUBLY

WELCOME TO YOUR NEW ADDICTION 🔥

Not another boring flat grid. This is a CUBE. All 6 faces. All your problems.

🎲 WHAT JUST HAPPENED?

  • Shapes fall. You drag them.
  • Fill a line → POOF! Gone.
  • Fill multiple lines → BIGGER POOF!
  • Run out of moves → Game over. Try again.

🤯 WHY YOU'LL BE OBSESSED

  • That SOUND when lines clear? *chef's kiss*
  • Spinning the cube feels illegal
  • "One more game" turns into 3am
  • Daily challenges (they change EVERY DAY)
  • No wifi needed. Play anywhere.

🚀 FOR THE COMPETITIVE

  • Beat your high score
  • Clear combos for bonus points
  • Hard shapes? Bring it on
  • Leaderboards coming soon

🔄 HOW IT WORKS (it's easy, promise)

  • Grab a shape
  • Put it on the cube
  • Make lines disappear
  • Try not to smile (you will)

Perfect for: Bus rides, bathroom breaks, pretending to work, proving you're smarter than your friends.
